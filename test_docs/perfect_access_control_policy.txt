# Access Control Policy
**Document ID:** SEC-AC-001
**Version:** 2.4
**Last Reviewed:** May 1, 2026
**Owner:** Head of Information Security

## 1. Purpose
The purpose of this policy is to establish strict guidelines for access control across all company systems, ensuring compliance with ISO 27001 and NIST frameworks.

## 2. Principle of Least Privilege
All user accounts, including administrative accounts, must be provisioned using the principle of least privilege. Users will only be granted the minimum access rights necessary to perform their job functions. 

## 3. Access Reviews
Access rights for all systems and applications must be reviewed formally on a quarterly basis by the system owner. Any orphaned accounts or unnecessary privileges must be revoked within 24 hours of the review.

## 4. Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)
Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is strictly mandated and technically enforced for all remote access to the corporate network, including VPNs, and for access to all critical systems handling customer data.

## 5. Termination Procedures
Upon termination of employment, HR must notify IT immediately. All access rights, physical tokens, and logical accounts must be disabled within 1 hour of the termination event.
