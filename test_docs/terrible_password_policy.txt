COMPANY PASSWORD GUIDELINES
Last Updated: March 2021

Employees should try to use a good password when logging into company systems. 

Passwords should probably have a number in them and we recommend not using your pet's name. You can change your password if you think someone else knows it, but we don't force you to change it regularly because that annoys people. 

If you forget your password, just email the IT helpdesk from your personal email and they will set it to "Password123" for you so you can log back in.

Please write your passwords down on a sticky note and keep it in your desk drawer, do not stick it directly to your monitor as that is a security risk.

We don't use 2FA right now because the CEO's phone is old and it doesn't work well for him.
