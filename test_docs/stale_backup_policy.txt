# Data Backup Policy
Version 1.0
Author: Sysadmin Dave
Date: October 14, 2022

## Backup Strategy
All core production databases are backed up to the secondary data center on a nightly basis at 2:00 AM EST.
The backup process uses standard pg_dump tools.

## Retention
Backups are retained on disk for 30 days. After 30 days, they are moved to cold tape storage and kept for 7 years.

## Testing
We will test the restoration of these backups at least once per year to ensure data integrity. The last test was performed in December 2022 and was successful.

## Cryptography
Backups are stored using AES-128 encryption. 
Note: We plan to upgrade to AES-256 sometime in 2024 when we upgrade the storage array.
