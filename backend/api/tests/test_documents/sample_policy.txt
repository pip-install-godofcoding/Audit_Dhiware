INFORMATION SECURITY POLICY
Version 2.1 — Effective January 2025

1. ACCESS CONTROL POLICY
An access control policy has been established and documented. Access to systems and applications 
is controlled by a secure log-on procedure including multi-factor authentication (MFA).
All user access rights are reviewed quarterly (every 90 days) by asset owners.

2. CRYPTOGRAPHIC CONTROLS
AES-256 encryption is used for all data at rest containing PII. TLS 1.3 is enforced for data in transit.
Key management procedures follow NIST SP 800-57 guidelines.

3. INCIDENT RESPONSE
Management responsibilities and procedures have been established to ensure quick, effective, 
and orderly response to information security incidents. The incident response team conducts 
tabletop exercises quarterly.

4. VULNERABILITY MANAGEMENT
Vulnerability scans are performed monthly using Nessus. Critical vulnerabilities are remediated 
within 48 hours. All findings are tracked in Jira.

5. BUSINESS CONTINUITY
Recovery Time Objective (RTO): 4 hours. Recovery Point Objective (RPO): 1 hour.
Business continuity plans are tested annually. Backups are verified weekly.

6. SECURE DEVELOPMENT
All developers follow OWASP Top 10 guidelines. Code reviews are mandatory before deployment.
Static analysis (SAST) runs in CI/CD pipeline. No known critical vulnerabilities in production.
